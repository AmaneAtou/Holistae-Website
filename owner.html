HOLISTAE OWNER PERFUMER PROFILE

Daniel VONACHEN

General Manager and owner of HOLISTÆ brand

Master perfumer and cosmetology professor

Project Manager Initiator of many business projects mostly in the field of Cosmetics and Perfume (FRANCE, Cannes / USA, Miami / AFRICA, Abidjan, / ASIA, Hong Kong./ VIETNAM Saigon)

Founder and Operator of cosmetics and perfume brands.

Professional Experience

From 1978 to 1982 - France / Paris: Several positions in the cosmetic industry, including BOURJOIS CHANEL. In parallel, attended ISIPCA Perfumery and Cosmetics School to obtain a Master’s degree in applied sciences with a Perfume option.

From 1982 to 1985 - France / Paris: Deputy Director of I.P.S.A PIERRE CARDIN (Institut Permanent Supérieur d’Animation) PARIS.

From 1985 to 1991 - Africa / Ivory Coast / Abidjan: Vice President of Ethnic Products at REVLON REALISTIC Inc., based in Abidjan, Ivory Coast, Africa.

From 1991 to 2001 - Africa / Ivory Coast / Abidjan: Founder and CEO of LABORATOIRE DERMATOLOGIQUE DE FRANCE (Labo.DERMA). Co-founder and manufacturer of the emblematic brand "FAIR&WHITE". Partner of the laboratory MEDICA CONTINENTAL in France.

From 2001 to 2005 - France / Cannes: Founder and CEO of Laboratoire DERMALYS in Antibes, France. Creator and manufacturer of brands such as "ORLEEN DE CANNES", "GREENFLOYD", "OPALEEN", and “KARMA Collection.”

From 2005 to 2011 - USA / Florida / Miami: Founder and General Manager of CAPTIVI Group Inc., Miami. Created, manufactured, and distributed cosmetics and perfumes. Designed high-quality perfumes for VIPs including "AKON", "LADY GAGA", "GALINA TODD", "Jennifer LOPEZ", "BEYONCE", "MOKOBE", and "Matt HAMILTON".

From 2011 to 2020 - France / Cannes & Hong Kong, China: Partner of SARL MARCUS SPURWAY, Grasse. Regional Sales Manager for Asia at LABORATOIRE CONTINENTAL and LABO.DERMA. Co-Creator of the "Delys" brand and the "SCENTORIAS – MARCUS SPURWAY" concept in Hong Kong. Founder of CAPTIVI Group Ltd, Hong Kong, importing and distributing "Marcus Spurway", "Delys", "Fair & White", and creating OEM brands.

Today, more distant from business, but not from perfume, he became a novelist and author of books on perfumery under the pseudonym

DAN ACHEN.

And offers his experience and know-how to VIETNAM, a magnificent country with which, and where, he fell in love.

HOLISTӔ est une marque Française qui a développé de nombreux produits dans les domaines de la parfumerie, des cosmétiques, des produits de beauté, de la mode et de l’art.
